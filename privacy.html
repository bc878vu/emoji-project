Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

EmojiSaaS is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identification data. There is no account registration required.

2. Browser Storage (LocalStorage)

We use LocalStorage on your device to store your "Favorites", "Recent" history, and "Theme Preference" (Dark/Light). This data stays entirely on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts to provide a professional typography experience. No other third-party tracking, cookies, or advertisement scripts are used on this platform.

4. Data Security

Since we do not store your data on any server, your information is as secure as your own computer or mobile device. Clearing your browser cache will remove your favorites and recent history.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this policy, feel free to contact us through the portfolio links provided in the footer.
